<p><p>Precision chip resistors are widely used in various electronic devices for accurate resistance values. There are two main types of precision chip resistors - thin film and thick film. Thin film resistors are made by depositing a thin film of resistive material onto a ceramic substrate, offering high precision and stability. Thick film resistors, on the other hand, have a thicker resistive layer, providing higher power handling capabilities. Both types cater to different market demands based on factors such as precision, stability, and power requirements.</p></p>
