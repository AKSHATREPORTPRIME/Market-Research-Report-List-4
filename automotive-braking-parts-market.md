<p><p>The automotive braking parts market consists of various types of components such as brake lines, disc brakes, drum brakes, brake calipers, and pistons. Brake lines are used to transmit hydraulic pressure to the brake calipers, which then squeeze the disc brakes to slow down the vehicle. Drum brakes work by pressing brake shoes against the inside of a rotating drum. Brake calipers contain pistons that push against the brake pads to create friction and slow down the vehicle.</p></p>
