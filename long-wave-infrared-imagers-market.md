<p><p>Long-Wave Infrared Imagers are categorized into two types: Unrefrigerated and Refrigerated. Unrefrigerated LWIR imagers do not require cooling systems, making them more cost-effective and portable, but they have lower sensitivity and are suitable for shorter-range applications. On the other hand, Refrigerated LWIR imagers use cooling systems to enhance sensitivity and are ideal for long-range applications requiring higher image quality. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in various industries such as defense, surveillance, and medical imaging.</p></p>
