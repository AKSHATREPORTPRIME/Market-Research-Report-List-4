<p><p>Surface Mount System Market encompasses various types such as Placement, Inspection, Soldering, Screen Printing, and Others. Placement systems are used for accurately placing electronic components onto printed circuit boards. Inspection systems are used to ensure the quality of the placement process. Soldering systems are responsible for creating secure electrical connections between components and PCBs. Screen printing systems are used for applying solder paste onto PCBs. Other systems in the market include cleaning, reflow, and rework systems that are essential for the overall SMT process.</p></p>
