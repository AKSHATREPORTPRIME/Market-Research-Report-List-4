<p><p>Full dentures are a type of dental prosthesis that are used to replace missing teeth and surrounding tissues. They are removable appliances that consist of a complete set of artificial teeth attached to a flesh-colored acrylic base. They provide functional and aesthetic benefits to individuals who have lost all of their natural teeth.</p><p>The Full Dentures Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 5.10% during the forecast period.
