Interleukin blockers target specific proteins involved in the immune response. Other drugs in the market may include corticosteroids, nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, and disease-modifying antirheumatic drugs. These medications help to manage symptoms and improve quality of life for patients with psoriatic arthritis.</p></p>
