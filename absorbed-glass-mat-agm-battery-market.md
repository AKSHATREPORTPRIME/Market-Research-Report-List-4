<p><p>An Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) Battery is a type of lead-acid battery that uses a fiberglass mat separator to absorb the electrolyte solution. This design allows the electrolyte to be held in place and prevents it from leaking out, even if the battery is broken. AGM batteries are known for their maintenance-free and spill-proof nature, making them popular choices for applications such as automotive starting, lighting, and ignition (SLI), electric vehicles, renewable energy storage, and UPS systems.</p><p>The AGM Battery Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for reliable and long-lasting batteries in various industries.
