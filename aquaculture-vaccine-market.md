<p><p>Aquaculture vaccine market includes three main types - Live vaccines, inactivated vaccines, and other vaccines. Live vaccines contain weakened or modified live pathogens that stimulate an immune response without causing the disease itself. Inactivated vaccines contain killed pathogens that also trigger an immune response. Other vaccines may include subunit vaccines, DNA vaccines, or recombinant vaccines. Each type of vaccine has its own advantages and disadvantages, depending on the specific disease being targeted and the desired immune response.</p></p>
