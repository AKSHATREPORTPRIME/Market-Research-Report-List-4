<p><p>The Disc Roller Mill market includes two main types: Spring Loaded and Hydraulic Pressure. Spring Loaded Disc Roller Mills use a spring mechanism to apply pressure between the rollers, while Hydraulic Pressure Disc Roller Mills use hydraulic systems to control the pressure. Both types offer efficient grain processing and can be utilized in various industries such as agriculture and food processing. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs of the application and desired level of control over the milling process.</p></p>
