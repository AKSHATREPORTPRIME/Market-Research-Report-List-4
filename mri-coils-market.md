<p><strong>The MRI Coils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phased-array</li><li>Quadrature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MRI coils are essential components used in magnetic resonance imaging to improve image quality and signal-to-noise ratio. Phased-array coils consist of multiple small coil elements arranged in a grid pattern, allowing for better spatial resolution and faster imaging. Quadrature coils utilize two coil elements positioned perpendicular to each other to produce two distinct signals, leading to improved image quality and better penetration depth. Both types of coils play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of MRI systems by capturing and transmitting signals effectively.</p></p>
