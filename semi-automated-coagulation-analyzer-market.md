<p><p>Semi-automated coagulation analyzers are widely available in two main types: benchtop and handheld. They offer increased capacity and throughput for processing multiple samples simultaneously. In contrast, handheld analyzers are portable, compact devices ideal for point-of-care testing in smaller clinics or ambulatory care settings. They provide quick results and greater flexibility for on-the-go testing, making them a convenient option for smaller facilities or remote locations.</p></p>
