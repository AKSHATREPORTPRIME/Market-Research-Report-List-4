<p><p>Froth Flotation Machine is a widely used process for separating valuable minerals from ores. There are two main types of Froth Flotation Machines: Cell-to-Cell Flotation Machine and Free-Flow Flotation Machine. Cell-to-Cell Flotation Machine involves individual cells connected to form a flotation circuit, while Free-Flow Flotation Machine allows for continuous operation without the need for individual cells. Both types offer efficient separation of minerals through the generation of bubbles and their attachment to specific minerals, ultimately leading to their recovery.</p></p>
