<p><p>Thermosiphon systems are commonly classified into two market types: circulation systems and direct systems. Circulation systems use a pump to circulate water between the collector and storage tank, providing more flexibility in installation and operation. Meanwhile, direct systems rely on natural convection to transfer heated water from the collector to the storage tank, making them simpler and more cost-effective. Both systems are popular choices for residential and commercial solar water heating applications due to their efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
