<p><p>High Temperature Recovery Wheels are used for heat and moisture recovery in ventilation systems. The market can be classified into three types based on their functionality: Condensation Rotors, which condense and recover heat from exhaust air; Hygroscopic Rotors, which absorb and release moisture for humidity control; and Sorption Rotors, which utilize a desiccant material to absorb and release moisture and recover heat. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications, catering to varying needs in the commercial and industrial sectors.</p></p>
