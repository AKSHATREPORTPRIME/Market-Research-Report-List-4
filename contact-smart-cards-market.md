<p><p>Contact smart cards can be categorized into two main types: active cards and passive cards. Active cards have their own power source and are capable of processing data on their own, making them suitable for tasks requiring high levels of security and authentication. On the other hand, passive cards rely on an external reader for power and data transmission, making them more cost-effective and suitable for applications where security requirements are not as high. Both types of cards play a crucial role in various industries such as banking, healthcare, and transportation.</p></p>
