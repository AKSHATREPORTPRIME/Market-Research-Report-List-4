<p><p>Extended release drug market consists of two main types: Sustained release drug and controlled release drug. Sustained release drugs slowly release the medication over an extended period of time, maintaining a constant drug concentration in the bloodstream. Controlled release drugs provide a more precise control over the rate and timing of drug release, allowing for targeted delivery of medication to specific areas of the body. Both types of extended release drugs offer benefits such as reduced dosing frequency and improved patient compliance.</p></p>
