<p><p>Power harrows are primarily categorized into three types based on their working mechanisms: reciprocating power harrow, horizontal rotation power harrow, and vertical rotation power harrow. Reciprocating power harrows use a back-and-forth motion to break up soil, while horizontal rotation power harrows feature blades that rotate parallel to the ground. Vertical rotation power harrows have blades that rotate perpendicular to the ground. Each type of power harrow has its own unique advantages and is suitable for different soil types and agricultural applications.</p></p>
