<p><p>The ash handling system market is categorized into two types: wet type and dry type. Wet type systems involve mixing ash with water to form slurry, which is then transported through pipelines or conveyor systems. On the other hand, dry type systems involve mechanical conveying or pneumatic conveying of ash without the use of water. Both types of systems have their own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the industry or power plant.</p></p>
