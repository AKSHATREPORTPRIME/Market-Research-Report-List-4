<p><p>Solar powered data buoys are commonly classified into two types: base market and tower market. Base market data buoys are equipped with sensors and communication systems that are directly mounted on the buoy's base, providing accurate and real-time data collection. On the other hand, tower market data buoys feature additional structures, such as towers or masts, to elevate sensors above the water surface for enhanced data accuracy. Both types of data buoys utilize solar power to operate efficiently and sustainably in remote marine environments.</p></p>
