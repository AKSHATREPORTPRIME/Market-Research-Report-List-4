<p><p>Desiccator cabinets are commonly categorized into two main types: Vacuum and Non-Vacuum. Vacuum desiccator cabinets utilize a vacuum pump to create a vacuum seal within the cabinet, which enhances the removal of moisture and creates a more efficient drying environment. On the other hand, Non-Vacuum desiccator cabinets rely on desiccants such as silica gel to absorb moisture from the air inside the cabinet. Both types of desiccator cabinets are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and laboratories for preserving sensitive materials and equipment.</p></p>
