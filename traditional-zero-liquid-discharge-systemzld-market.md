<p><p>Traditional Zero Liquid Discharge System (ZLD) includes four main market types: Preprocessing, Filter, Evaporation, and Crystallization. Preprocessing involves treating wastewater before it enters the ZLD system. Filters are used to remove solid particles from the water. Evaporation is a process where water is heated to create steam, leaving behind concentrated brine. Crystallization involves further concentrating the brine to separate out salts. These market types work together to achieve zero liquid discharge by treating and reusing wastewater effectively.</p></p>
