<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Regenerative Braking System market can be categorized into three main types: Electric, Hydraulic, and Other. The Electric type utilizes an electric motor to slow down the vehicle and generate electricity, which is then stored in the battery for later use. The Hydraulic type uses hydraulic fluid to transfer energy from the braking system to a hydraulic accumulator. The Other market includes unique systems that may use different technologies such as mechanical or pneumatic to achieve regenerative braking in electric vehicles.</p></p>
