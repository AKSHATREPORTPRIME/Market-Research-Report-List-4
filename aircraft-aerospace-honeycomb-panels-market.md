These panels are commonly used in aircraft interiors, cabinetry, and flooring due to their lightweight and high strength properties. </p><p>Curved Laminated Honeycomb Panels are similar to flat panels but are designed to be curved or shaped to fit specific applications. These panels are used in areas where flat panels may not be suitable, such as in aircraft fuselages or wings.</p></p>
