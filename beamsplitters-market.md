<p><ul><li>Plate Type</li><li>Pellicle Type</li><li>Cube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beamsplitters are devices that split a single beam of light into two or more beams. There are three main types of beamsplitters in the market; Plate type, Pellicle type, and Cube type. </p><p>- Plate type beamsplitters are thin glass plates coated with a partially reflective coating that splits light by reflecting a portion and transmitting the rest.</p><p>- Pellicle type beamsplitters are thin membranes that split light by partially reflecting it and are commonly used in applications requiring minimal light loss.</p><p>- Cube type beamsplitters are made up of two right-angle prisms cemented together with a partially reflective coating on the diagonal face.</p></p>
