<p><strong>The Beer Dispensing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Draw System</li><li>Air Cooled System</li><li>Glycol Cooled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beer dispensing systems come in various types to cater to different needs. Direct draw systems are the most common and simplest, with the beer kegs stored directly below the tap. Air-cooled systems use forced air to keep beer lines cool, while glycol-cooled systems use a separate glycol chiller to maintain the temperature of beer lines over longer distances. Each system has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors like space availability, distance between keg and tap, and desired temperature control.</p></p>
