<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Energy efficient powder coatings are classified into two main types - thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic powder coatings are applied in a fluid state and are then heated to liquefy and form a durable finish. They can be melted and reshaped multiple times without losing their properties. On the other hand, thermosetting powder coatings undergo a chemical reaction during curing, forming a permanent cross-linked network that provides excellent durability and resistance to heat and chemicals. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
