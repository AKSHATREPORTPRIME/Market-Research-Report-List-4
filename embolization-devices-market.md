Embolic Coils are used to block blood flow in vessels, Liquid Agents dissolve clots, EPDs prevent emboli from travelling during procedures, Flow Diverters redirect blood flow, and Access Devices help in gaining access to blood vessels. Each of these devices plays a crucial role in treating conditions such as aneurysms, arteriovenous malformations, and other vascular disorders.</p></p>
