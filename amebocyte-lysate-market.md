<p><p>Amebocyte lysate is a blood derivative used in the detection of bacterial endotoxins. There are two main types of amebocyte lysate on the market: Limulus amebocyte lysate (LAL) and Tachypleus amebocyte lysate (TAL). LAL is derived from the blood of the Atlantic horseshoe crab while TAL is derived from the blood of the Chinese horseshoe crab. Both types of lysate are sensitive to bacterial endotoxins and are widely used in the pharmaceutical and medical device industries for quality control purposes.</p></p>
