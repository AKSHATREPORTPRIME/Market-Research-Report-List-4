<p><p>Thermal actuators are devices that convert thermal energy into mechanical motion. The three main types of thermal actuators are squeeze type, diaphragm type, and piston type. </p><p>Squeeze type actuators use thermal expansion to squeeze a fluid or material, causing mechanical motion. Diaphragm type actuators use a diaphragm that expands or contracts with temperature changes to generate movement. Piston type actuators utilize a piston that moves in response to thermal expansion or contraction.</p><p>These different types of thermal actuators are used in various industrial applications for precise control and actuation.</p></p>
