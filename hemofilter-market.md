Factors driving this growth include the increasing prevalence of kidney diseases, rising geriatric population, and technological advancements in hemodialysis equipment. </p><p>One of the latest trends in the hemofilter market is the growing demand for portable and home hemodialysis devices, allowing patients to receive treatment in the comfort of their own homes. <p><p>Hemofilter market is divided into two main types: portable hemofilter and conventional hemofilter. Portable hemofilters are smaller in size, easy to move around, and typically used in emergency or ambulatory care settings. They offer flexibility and mobility to patients needing continuous renal replacement therapy. On the other hand, conventional hemofilters are larger machines commonly used in hospital settings for patients with acute kidney injury or end-stage renal disease. They are more powerful and have higher capacity for treating a wider range of conditions.</p></p>
