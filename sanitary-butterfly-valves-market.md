<p><p>Sanitary butterfly valves are available in two main market types: manual and actuated. Manual butterfly valves are operated by hand using a handle or hand wheel, making them simple and cost-effective for smaller-scale operations. On the other hand, actuated butterfly valves are equipped with an automatic actuator that allows for remote or automated operation, making them ideal for larger-scale or industrial applications. Both types offer reliable and efficient control of flow in sanitary systems, with actuated valves providing additional convenience and versatility.</p></p>
