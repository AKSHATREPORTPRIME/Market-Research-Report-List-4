<p><p>Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are mechanical pumps used to support heart function in patients with severe heart failure. There are three main types of VADs: Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs) which assist the left side of the heart, Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs) which assist the right side of the heart, and Biventricular Assist Devices (BIVADs) which assist both sides of the heart. Each type of VAD is used depending on the specific needs and condition of the patient to help improve heart function and overall quality of life.</p></p>
