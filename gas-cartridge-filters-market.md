<p><p>Gas cartridge filters are used in various industries to remove impurities from gas streams. Two common types of filters are surface filters and depth filters. Surface filters are designed to capture particles on the surface of the filter medium, while depth filters trap particles within the thickness of the medium. Surface filters are ideal for removing larger particles, while depth filters are effective at removing smaller particles. Both types of filters are important for maintaining clean and safe gas streams in industrial applications.</p></p>
