<p><p>Industrial Water Purifier market consists of three main types: Reverse Osmosis Purifier (RO), Ultraviolet Rays Purifier (UV), and Ultrafiltration (UF). RO purifiers use a semipermeable membrane to remove impurities, UV purifiers use ultraviolet rays to kill bacteria and viruses, and UF purifiers use a fine membrane to filter out particles. Each type offers different levels of filtration and purification, catering to various industrial water treatment needs. Overall, these technologies play a crucial role in providing clean and safe water for industrial use.</p></p>
