<p><p>Steel Core Aluminum Alloy Stranded Wire Market includes two main types: ACSR/AW and ACSR/TW. ACSR/AW stands for Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced/Alloy Wire, which features a combination of aluminum alloy strands for conductivity and a steel core for strength. On the other hand, ACSR/TW stands for Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced/Tin Wire, which includes a tin-coated steel core for enhanced corrosion resistance. These wire types are widely used in overhead power transmission and distribution lines due to their high strength-to-weight ratio and durability.</p></p>
