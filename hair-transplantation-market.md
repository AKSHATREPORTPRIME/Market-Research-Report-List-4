<p><strong>The Hair Transplantation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hair transplantation market can be categorized into two main types: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves removing a strip of skin from the back of the scalp and dissecting it into individual follicular units for transplantation. On the other hand, FUE involves extracting individual follicular units directly from the scalp using a punch tool. Both methods are popular choices for those seeking to restore their hairline and combat baldness.</p></p>
