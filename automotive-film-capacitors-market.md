<p><p>Automotive Film Capacitors are categorized into different types based on the type of film material used in their construction. PET Capacitors are made from polyethylene terephthalate, PEN Capacitors from polyethylene naphthalate, PP Capacitors from polypropylene, and PPS Capacitors from polyphenylene sulphide. Each type offers specific characteristics and benefits, such as high thermal stability, low dielectric absorption, and excellent moisture resistance. These capacitors are widely used in automotive applications for various electrical functions due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
