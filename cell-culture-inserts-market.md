<p><p>Cell culture inserts are essential tools for research in cell biology and drug development. The three main types of inserts include PTFE membrane, mixed cellulose esters membrane, and polycarbonate membrane. PTFE membranes offer low protein binding and are suitable for applications requiring high chemical resistance. Mixed cellulose esters membranes provide good visibility under a microscope and are ideal for imaging studies. Polycarbonate membranes are durable and provide optimal cell attachment. Researchers can choose the appropriate insert based on the specific requirements of their experiments.</p></p>
