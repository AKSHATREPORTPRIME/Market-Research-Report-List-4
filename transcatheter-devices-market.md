TMVR (Transcatheter Mitral Valve Replacement) and TMVI (Transcatheter Mitral Valve Implantation) are used for the same purpose for the mitral valve. These devices offer a less invasive alternative to traditional valve replacement surgeries, resulting in shorter recovery times and improved outcomes for patients.</p></p>
