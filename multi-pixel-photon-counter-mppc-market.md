<p><p>A Multi Pixel Photon Counter (MPPC) is a semiconductor device that is used to detect low-level light signals. On the other hand, in Multiphoton Detection, MPPCs are used to detect multiple photons simultaneously, enabling applications such as fluorescence lifetime imaging microscopy and time-resolved spectroscopy. These diverse market types demonstrate the versatility and efficiency of MPPC technology in photon detection.</p></p>
