<p><p>Air injectors are a crucial component of modern fuel injection systems. Two common types of air injectors in the market are multi-port injectors and single-port injectors. Multi-port injectors deliver fuel to each cylinder through individual ports, allowing for precise fuel distribution and optimized performance. In contrast, single-port injectors deliver fuel through a single port to all cylinders simultaneously. Both types play vital roles in ensuring efficient and effective fuel delivery in vehicles, with each offering unique advantages based on specific engine requirements.</p></p>
