<p><p>Inkjet heads are classified into two main types: Thermal MEMS Inkjet Heads and Piezo MEMS Inkjet Heads. Thermal MEMS Inkjet Heads use heat to create vapor bubbles that propel ink onto the substrate, while Piezo MEMS Inkjet Heads use piezoelectric elements to create pressure and eject ink droplets. Both types are widely used in the printing industry for their precision and high-quality output. The market for these inkjet heads is competitive, with manufacturers constantly innovating to improve performance and efficiency.</p></p>
