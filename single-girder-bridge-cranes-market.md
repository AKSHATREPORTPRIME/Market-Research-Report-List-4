The two main types of single girder bridge cranes are gantry cranes and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes have two uprights supporting the bridge beam, while half-gantry cranes have one upright and utilize a building column or wall for support on one end. Both types of cranes offer efficient material handling solutions in various industries like manufacturing, construction, and logistics.</p></p>
