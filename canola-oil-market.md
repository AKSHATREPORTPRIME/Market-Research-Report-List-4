<p><p>Canola oil is available in two main market types: cold-pressed canola oil and extracted canola oil. Cold-pressed canola oil is produced by pressing the seeds of the canola plant to extract the oil, maintaining its natural flavor and nutrients. Extracted canola oil, on the other hand, is produced using a chemical extraction process, resulting in a more refined product with a higher smoke point. Both types of canola oil are widely used in cooking and food production due to their mild flavor and health benefits.</p></p>
