There are three main types of enthalpy wheels: Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation Rotors utilize a condensation process to transfer heat, Hygroscopic Rotors use a moisture-absorbing material to transfer moisture, and Sorption Rotors use a desiccant material to remove moisture from the air. Each type of enthalpy wheel has its own unique benefits and applications.</p></p>
