<p><p>Power tool brushes come in various types based on the materials used in their construction. Electrographitic brushes are made of a combination of graphite and copper, known for their high conductivity and durability. Soft graphite brushes are softer and provide low sparking, ideal for delicate applications. Metal graphite brushes have a higher metal content, providing better wear resistance. Other types of brushes include carbon brushes, resin-bonded brushes, and powdered metal brushes, each offering unique characteristics to suit different power tool applications.</p></p>
