<p><p>Foot orthosis (FO) market is divided into two main types: Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to provide support and control through movement, making them ideal for athletes or individuals with active lifestyles. In contrast, static orthotics are designed to offer support and stability while standing or walking, making them suitable for individuals with conditions such as flat feet or plantar fasciitis. Both types of orthotics aim to improve foot function, reduce pain, and prevent injuries.</p></p>
