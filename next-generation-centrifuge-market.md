<p><p>The Next Generation Centrifuge market consists of two main types: Refrigerated Centrifuge and Ambient Centrifuge. Refrigerated Centrifuges are specifically designed to maintain low temperatures during centrifugation processes, making them ideal for applications requiring sample preservation and cooling. On the other hand, Ambient Centrifuges operate at room temperature and are suitable for general laboratory applications where sample temperature control is not a priority. Both types offer high-speed spinning capabilities for efficient separation of materials based on density differences.</p></p>
