<p><strong>The Pallet Pooling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tray static leasing</li><li>Tray dynamic flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pallet pooling market types include tray static leasing and tray dynamic flow market. Tray static leasing involves companies leasing a specific number of trays for a fixed period of time, allowing for consistent availability of trays for their operations. On the other hand, tray dynamic flow market allows for trays to be used and exchanged among multiple participants in a continuous flow, based on demand and supply. Both types offer flexibility and cost-effectiveness in managing pallet needs within the supply chain.</p></p>
