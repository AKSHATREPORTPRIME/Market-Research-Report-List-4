<p><ul><li>Plano-Concave</li><li>Plano-Convex</li><li>Concave-Convex</li><li>Aspheric Germanium Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Germanium lenses are available in various types, including Plano-Concave, Plano-Convex, Concave-Convex, and Aspheric lenses. Plano-Concave lenses have one flat and one inward curved surface, while Plano-Convex lenses have one flat and one outward curved surface. Concave-Convex lenses have both surfaces curved, with one inward and one outward. Aspheric lenses have a non-spherical surface to correct aberrations. Each type is used in different applications based on their specific optical properties and capabilities.</p></p>
