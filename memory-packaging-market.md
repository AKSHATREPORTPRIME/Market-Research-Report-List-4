<p><ul><li>Flip-chip</li><li>Lead-frame</li><li>Through-Silicon Via</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Memory packaging market encompasses various types including Flip-chip, Lead-frame, Through-Silicon Via, and others. Flip-chip packaging involves mounting the chip face-down on the substrate, offering high performance and space efficiency. Lead-frame packaging uses a metal frame to support the chip and connect it to the external circuit. Through-Silicon Via packaging involves passing the electrical signals through the silicon substrate. Other types may include Ball Grid Array, Chip Scale Package, and Dual In-line Package, each offering unique benefits in terms of performance, size, and cost.</p></p>
