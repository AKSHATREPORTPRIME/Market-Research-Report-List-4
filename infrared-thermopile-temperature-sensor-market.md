<p><p>Infrared Thermopile Temperature Sensor market includes two main types: Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensor and SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensor. Through Hole Thermopile Infrared Sensors are larger in size and are mounted through holes on the circuit board, providing easy access for soldering. On the other hand, SMD Thermopile Infrared Sensors are smaller and surface-mounted directly onto the circuit board, making them more suitable for compact and space-constrained applications. Both types offer accurate temperature sensing capabilities in various electronic devices.</p></p>
