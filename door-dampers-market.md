There are two main types of door dampers: Surface Applied Door Dampers, which are mounted on the surface of the door or frame, and Concealed Door Dampers, which are hidden within the door or frame. Surface applied dampers are easy to install and adjust, while concealed dampers provide a more seamless and aesthetic look. Both types help improve safety and minimize damage to doors and surrounding structures.</p></p>
