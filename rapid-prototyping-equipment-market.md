<p><p>Rapid Prototyping Equipment market is categorized into different types based on the technology used. Stereolithogrphy Apparatus (SLA) uses UV light to cure liquid resin layer by layer. Three Dimension Printing (3DP) creates objects by selectively depositing a binding material onto powder bed. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses laser to bind powdered material together. Laminated Object Manufacturing (LOM) involves cutting and bonding paper, plastic, or metal materials. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) constructs objects by extruding molten material. Other types include Digital Light Processing (DLP) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM).</p></p>
