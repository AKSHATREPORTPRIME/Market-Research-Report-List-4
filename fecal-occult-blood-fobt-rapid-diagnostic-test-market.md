<p><p>Fecal Occult Blood Rapid Diagnostic Test market includes two main types: Immunochemical Fecal Occult Blood Test (iFOBT or FIT) and Guaiac Fecal Occult Blood Test (gFOBT). iFOBT uses antibodies to detect blood in the stool, providing more accurate results and easier interpretation. On the other hand, gFOBT uses a chemical reaction to detect blood, but it may lead to false positives from dietary factors. Both tests are used for the early detection of colorectal cancer and other gastrointestinal disorders.</p></p>
