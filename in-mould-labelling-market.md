<p><p>In Mould Labelling (IML) is used in various markets such as Injection Molding, Extrusion-Blow Molding, and Thermoforming. Injection Molding involves the process of creating plastic parts by injecting molten material into a mold. Extrusion-Blow Molding is a manufacturing process where plastic is melted and extruded into a hollow tube, which is then formed into a specific shape. Thermoforming is a process where a plastic sheet is heated and formed into a specific shape using a mold. Each market type has its unique applications and advantages.</p></p>
