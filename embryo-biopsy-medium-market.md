<p><p>Embryo biopsy medium is a specialized culture medium used in pre-implantation genetic testing of embryos. This medium is used during the process of extracting a small number of cells from the embryo for genetic analysis, often to screen for genetic disorders or chromosomal abnormalities. The medium helps to maintain the viability of the embryo while the biopsy is being conducted.</p><p>The embryo biopsy medium market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for pre-implantation genetic testing in assisted reproductive technology procedures, as well as advancements in genetic screening technologies. Technological advancements have made it easier for laboratories to conduct genetic testing on embryos, driving the demand for embryo biopsy medium.</p><p>The latest trends in the embryo biopsy medium market include the development of more advanced culture media formulations that support embryo health and viability during the biopsy process. Additionally, there is a growing emphasis on the use of non-invasive genetic screening methods, which may impact the demand for traditional embryo biopsy medium in the future. These trends are expected to shape the growth of the embryo biopsy medium market in the years to come.</p></p>
