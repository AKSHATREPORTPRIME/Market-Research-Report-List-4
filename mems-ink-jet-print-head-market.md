<p><strong>The MEMS Ink Jet Print Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Print Head</li><li>Piezo Print Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal print heads use heat to eject ink onto the paper, while piezo print heads use electrically generated pressure to do so. Both types of print heads are commonly used in MEMS inkjet printers. Thermal print heads are known for their high speed and precision, making them ideal for applications requiring high-quality output. Piezo print heads are preferred for their durability and ability to handle a wider range of inks. The choice between the two often depends on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
