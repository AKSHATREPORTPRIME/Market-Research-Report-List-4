<p><p>Authentication Services market includes two main types: Single-Factor Authentication and Multi-Factor Authentication. Single-Factor Authentication relies on one method of verification, such as a password or fingerprint scan, making it less secure but more user-friendly. On the other hand, Multi-Factor Authentication requires multiple layers of validation, such as a password, SMS code, and fingerprint scan, providing higher security but potentially causing inconvenience for users. Both types cater to different needs in the market, offering varying levels of security and usability.</p></p>
