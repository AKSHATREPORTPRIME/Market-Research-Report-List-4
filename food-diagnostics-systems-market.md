<p><p>Food diagnostics systems market includes different types such as Chromatography, Spectrometry, Biosensor, and Immunoassay. Chromatography is used for separating and analyzing food components. Spectrometry uses light to identify and quantify molecules in food samples. Biosensors detect biological responses to identify contaminants in food. Immunoassays detect the presence of specific substances in food using antibodies. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring food safety by detecting contaminants, pathogens, and allergens in food products.</p></p>
