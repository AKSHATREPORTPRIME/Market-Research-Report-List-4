<p><ul><li>Continuous Lasers Systems</li><li>Pulsed Lasers Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Lasers Systems are devices that emit a continuous beam of light, providing a consistent output of energy. They are commonly used in applications requiring steady, uninterrupted delivery of laser energy, such as laser cutting or welding. </p><p>Pulsed Lasers Systems, on the other hand, emit short pulses of high-energy light with rest periods in between. They are often used in applications requiring high peak power, such as material processing or medical procedures. Both types of laser systems have unique advantages and are utilized in a variety of industries.</p></p>
