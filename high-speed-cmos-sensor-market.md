<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The High Speed CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS</li><li>Backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Speed CMOS Sensor market comprises front-illuminated (FI) CMOS sensors and backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensors. Front-illuminated (FI) CMOS sensors have the light-sensitive surface placed in front of the electronics, allowing for high sensitivity but lower quantum efficiency. On the other hand, backside-illuminated (BSI) CMOS sensors have the electronics located behind the light-sensitive surface, resulting in higher quantum efficiency and improved image quality, making them suitable for high-speed imaging applications. Both types cater to different needs in the high-speed CMOS sensor market.</p></p>
