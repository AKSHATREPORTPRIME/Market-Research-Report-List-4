<p><p>Blast Furnace market is commonly categorized into two types based on the method of supplying air to the furnace: Cold Blast Blowers and Hot Blast Ovens. Cold Blast Blowers use unheated air, which is blown into the furnace to aid combustion and reduce fuel consumption. On the other hand, Hot Blast Ovens preheat the air before it enters the furnace, increasing efficiency and reducing energy costs. Both types play a crucial role in the steelmaking industry by production process by providing the necessary air supply to the blast furnace.</p></p>
