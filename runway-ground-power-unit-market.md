<p><p>A Runway Ground Power Unit (GPU) is a mobile or fixed power supply used to provide ground power to aircraft while they are parked on the tarmac. This helps prevent the unnecessary use of aircraft engines and reduces fuel consumption and emissions, ultimately cutting down on aircraft operating costs and environmental impact.</p><p>The Runway Ground Power Unit Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
