<p><p>DC isolators are used to safely disconnect the power supply to a system for maintenance or emergency situations. Single break isolators have one breaking mechanism, while double break isolators have two for increased safety. Pantograph type isolators operate by moving a hinged arm up and down to establish or break the connection. Each type of isolator has its advantages and is used in different applications based on the specific needs of the system.</p></p>
