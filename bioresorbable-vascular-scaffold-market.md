<p><ul><li>Metal BVS</li><li>Polymer BVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bioresorbable vascular scaffold (BVS) market consists of two main types: metal BVS and polymer BVS. Metal BVS is made of materials like magnesium or zinc that gradually dissolve in the body over time, providing support to the blood vessel before being absorbed. Polymer BVS, on the other hand, is made of biodegradable polymers that serve a similar purpose of providing temporary structural support to the blood vessel before breaking down and being absorbed by the body. Both types offer potential advantages in terms of reducing long-term complications compared to traditional metal stents.</p></p>
