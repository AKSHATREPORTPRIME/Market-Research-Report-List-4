<p><p>The Lithium-ion battery equipment market is categorized into three main types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment equipment prepares raw materials for battery production, such as mixing and grinding of electrode materials. Cell assembly equipment is used to stack electrodes, separators, and electrolytes to form battery cells. Post-processing equipment is responsible for finishing and testing the assembled battery cells. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall manufacturing process of Lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
