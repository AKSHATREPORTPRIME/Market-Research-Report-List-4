<p><p>Solder Paste Inspection (SPI) System includes two main types: In-line SPI System and Off-line SPI System. In-line SPI System is integrated into the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of solder paste deposits during the assembly process. Off-line SPI System, on the other hand, is a standalone system used for post-production inspection of solder paste quality. Both types of SPI systems play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic assemblies by detecting defects in solder paste deposits.</p></p>
