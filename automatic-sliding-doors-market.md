<p><p>Automatic sliding doors come in two main types - Bi-Parting and Single Sliding. Bi-Parting doors have two panels that slide apart from the center to each side, providing a wider opening for high-traffic areas. Single sliding doors have one panel that slides to the side, ideal for smaller spaces or where limited space is available for the door to open. Both types offer convenience, efficiency, and safety in various commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
