<p><p>Ophthalmic Operating Microscope market can be classified into two main types based on the location of the light source. Inside Light Source Microscopes have the light source integrated within the microscope, offering convenience and ease of use. On the other hand, Outside Light Source Microscopes have a separate light source connected externally to the microscope. While Inside Light Source Microscopes are more compact and easier to maneuver, Outside Light Source Microscopes offer greater flexibility in terms of adjusting the intensity and angle of the light source.</p></p>
