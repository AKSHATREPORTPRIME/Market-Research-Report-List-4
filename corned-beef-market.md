<p><p>Corned beef is available in two main market types: flat cut and point cut. Flat cut corned beef is leaner and comes from the round primal cut of beef, resulting in a more uniform slice with less fat marbling. Point cut corned beef comes from the brisket primal cut and has more fat marbling, making it more flavorful and tender. Both cuts are popular for different preferences in taste and texture, with flat cut corned beef being preferred for its leanness and point cut for its rich flavor.</p></p>
