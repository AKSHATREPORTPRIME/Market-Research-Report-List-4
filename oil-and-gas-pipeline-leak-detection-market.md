<p><strong>The Oil and Gas Pipeline Leak Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass-Volume Balance</li><li>Acoustic/Ultrasonic</li><li>Fiber Optics</li><li>Vapor Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil and gas pipeline leak detection market includes various types of technologies. Mass-volume balance method uses sensors to measure changes in flow rate to detect leaks. Acoustic/ultrasonic technology detects leaks by analyzing sound waves generated by the escaping fluids. Fiber optics technology monitors changes in light signals caused by leaks. Vapor sensing technology detects leaks by analyzing changes in gas concentrations. Each of these technologies has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different types of pipelines and environments.</p></p>
