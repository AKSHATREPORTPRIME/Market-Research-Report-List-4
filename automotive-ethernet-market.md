<p><ul><li>Automotive Local Area Network (LAN)</li><li>Automotive Metropolitan Area Network (MAN)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Ethernet is segmented into two main types: Automotive Local Area Network (LAN) and Automotive Metropolitan Area Network (MAN). LAN refers to the communication network within a vehicle, connecting various electronic control units and sensors to facilitate data exchange and control functions. MAN, on the other hand, extends the network to cover a larger area, enabling communication between vehicles or between vehicles and infrastructure. Both LAN and MAN play a crucial role in enabling advanced driver assistance systems, autonomous driving, and connected car technology in the automotive industry.</p></p>
