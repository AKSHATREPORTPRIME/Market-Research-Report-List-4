<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) market consists of four types- Four Channels ABS, Three Channels ABS, Two Channels ABS, and Single Channel ABS. Four Channels ABS provides individual control over all four wheels, offering precise braking in emergency situations. Three Channels ABS controls one channel for two wheels, perfect for vehicles with dual rear wheels. Two Channels ABS controls two channels for all four wheels, ideal for smaller commercial vehicles. Single Channel ABS controls one channel for all wheels, suitable for motorcycles and scooters.</p></p>
