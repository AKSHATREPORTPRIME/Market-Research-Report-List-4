Fiber Laser is known for high beam quality and precision, making it ideal for marking metals and plastics. Diode Laser is cost-effective and commonly used for marking applications requiring lower power. Solid State Laser is versatile and can be used for various materials. CO2 Laser is preferred for marking organic materials like paper, wood, and plastics. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
