<p><p>PCB pin insertion machines come in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually insert pins into the PCB. Semi-automatic machines partially automate the process, with operators inserting pins while the machine handles other tasks. Fully automatic machines automate the entire pin insertion process without human intervention. Each type of machine offers varying degrees of efficiency and productivity, catering to different levels of production requirements in the PCB manufacturing industry.</p></p>
