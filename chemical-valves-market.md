<p><p>Chemical valves market can be categorized into various types based on their specific functions. Gate valves are used to control the flow of liquids in a pipeline. Control valves regulate the flow rate of liquids or gases. Ball valves use a spherical disc to control flow. Butterfly valves have a rotating disc to regulate flow. Other types of valves include diaphragm valves, pinch valves, and needle valves, each serving different purposes in the chemical industry. These valves are essential for controlling the movement of chemicals in industrial processes.</p></p>
