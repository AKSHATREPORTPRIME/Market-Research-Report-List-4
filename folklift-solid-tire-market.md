<p><p>The forklift solid tire market consists of two main types: Cured-on solid tires and Pressed-on solid tires. Cured-on solid tires are molded and cured onto the wheel rim in a manufacturing process, providing a durable and long-lasting tire option. Pressed-on solid tires are attached to the wheel rim through a pressing process, offering easy installation and replacement. Both types of solid tires are popular choices for forklifts, providing reliable performance and reduced maintenance costs for industrial applications.</p></p>
