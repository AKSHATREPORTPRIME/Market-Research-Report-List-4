<p><p>Pharmaceutical starch market is divided into three main types: Corn Starch, Potato Starch, and Others. Corn starch is commonly used in pharmaceuticals due to its smooth texture and binding properties. Potato starch is preferred for its high viscosity and clarity. Other types of pharmaceutical starch include rice starch, tapioca starch, and wheat starch, which offer unique properties for different applications. Overall, these starches play a crucial role in pharmaceutical formulations as binders, disintegrants, or fillers to ensure the effectiveness and quality of medicinal products.</p></p>
