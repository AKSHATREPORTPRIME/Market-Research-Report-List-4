<p><p>Piezoresistive accelerometers are devices used to measure acceleration in various applications. They come in different types, namely 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis accelerometers. </p><p>1-axis accelerometers measure acceleration in a single direction, providing precise data on motion along one axis. </p><p>2-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along two perpendicular axes, providing data on both horizontal and vertical movements. </p><p>3-axis accelerometers feature multiple sensors to measure acceleration along three axes, offering comprehensive data on motion in any direction. These different types cater to a variety of applications and industries.</p></p>
