<p><p>Piezoelectric print heads and thermal print heads are two main types of print heads used in production printers. Piezoelectric print heads use electrical signals to create droplets of ink, offering high precision and consistency. On the other hand, thermal print heads use heat to vaporize ink, resulting in faster printing speeds. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific printing needs, such as quality, speed, and cost-effectiveness. The market for these print heads is competitive and continuously evolving to meet the demands of the printing industry.</p></p>
