<p><p>In-Mold Labelling (IML) is a process where labels are placed in the mold before the plastic material is injected, blown, or thermoformed to create a finished product with the label embedded in it. In injection molding, the label is placed in the mold cavity before the plastic is injected. In blow molding, the label is inserted into the parison before it is blown into the desired shape. In thermoforming, the label is placed on the sheet before it is formed into the final product.</p></p>
