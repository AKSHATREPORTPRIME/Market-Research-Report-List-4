<p><p>Immersion parts washers are categorized into two main types: solvent parts washers and aqueous parts washers. Solvent parts washers use cleaning solvents like mineral spirits or petroleum-based fluids to remove grease, oil, and other contaminants from parts. Aqueous parts washers, on the other hand, use water-based detergents and heated water to clean parts without the use of harsh chemicals. Both types of immersion parts washers offer effective cleaning solutions for industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and aerospace.</p></p>
