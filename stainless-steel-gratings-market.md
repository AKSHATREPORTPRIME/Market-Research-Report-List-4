<p><ul><li>Welded Grating</li><li>Swage Locked Grating</li><li>Press Locked Grating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stainless steel gratings are available in various types, including welded grating, swage locked grating, press locked grating, and others. Welded gratings are constructed through the fusion of bearing bars and cross bars. Swage locked gratings feature a high-strength construction with no welds, while press locked gratings use pressure to secure bars together. Other types of gratings may include riveted gratings or close mesh gratings. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of strength, durability, and application suitability.</p></p>
