<p><p>Disposable dental articulators are designed for temporary use in dental clinics for dental impressions and wax shaping. Three main types include flat type, slot type, and oblique type. Flat type articulators are simple and cost-effective, ideal for basic procedures. Slot type articulators have slots for better stability and support during procedures. Oblique type articulators have angled structures for precise positioning of dental models. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of dental professionals, offering flexibility and convenience in dental procedures.</p></p>
