<p><strong>The Bottled Mineral Water Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Mineral Water</li><li>Artificial Mineral Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural mineral water is sourced from underground mineral springs and contains various minerals and trace elements naturally present in the water. It is usually unaltered and filtered for purity. Artificial mineral water, on the other hand, is water that has been treated and enhanced with minerals and electrolytes to mimic the composition of natural mineral water. Both types of bottled mineral water cater to consumers looking for a healthier alternative to regular tap water, with natural mineral water being preferred for its authenticity and purity.</p></p>
