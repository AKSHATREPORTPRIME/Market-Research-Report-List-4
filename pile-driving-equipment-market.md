<p><strong>The Pile Driving Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Pile Drivers</li><li>Piling Hammers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pile driving equipment market consists of two main types: static pile drivers and piling hammers. Static pile drivers use a large weight to drive piles into the ground using the force of gravity, making them suitable for soft to medium soil conditions. Piling hammers, on the other hand, use hydraulic or diesel power to deliver repeated blows to drive piles into the ground, making them more effective in hard soil conditions. Both types are essential in construction projects requiring foundation support.</p></p>
