<p><p>Large molecule bioanalytical testing services are categorized into preclinical, with antibody, without antibody, and clinical markets. With antibody testing focuses on analyzing how large molecules interact with specific antibodies. Without antibody testing involves measuring the concentration and activity of large molecules without the use of specific antibodies. Clinical testing is conducted on human subjects to assess the safety and effectiveness of large molecules for potential therapeutic use.</p></p>
