<p><p>Tunnel liner market can be broadly categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I liners are specifically designed for tunnels excavated in rock formations, providing high durability and resistance to water infiltration. On the other hand, Type II liners are suitable for tunnels constructed in soft soil or unstable ground, offering flexibility and high tensile strength. Both types of liners play a crucial role in ensuring structural integrity and longevity of tunnels, meeting the diverse requirements of different tunnel construction projects.</p></p>
