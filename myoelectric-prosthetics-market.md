<p><p>Myoelectric prosthetics market is divided into upper limb and lower limb segments. Upper limb prosthetics are designed for individuals who have lost part or all of their arm, hand, or fingers, allowing them to regain function and control through signals from the muscles in their residual limb. Lower limb prosthetics, on the other hand, are designed for individuals who have lost part or all of their leg, allowing them to walk, run, and perform daily activities with ease and stability. Both types aim to improve the quality of life for amputees.</p></p>
