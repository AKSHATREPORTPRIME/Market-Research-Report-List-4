<p><p>Fully Automated Liquid Handling Workstation Market consists of two main types: Non-contact Liquid Handling Workstation and Contact Type Liquid Handling Workstation. </p><p>Non-contact Liquid Handling Workstation utilizes technologies like acoustic droplet ejection or surface tension to dispense liquids without physically touching them. Contact Type Liquid Handling Workstation, on the other hand, uses physical contact such as pipette tips to transfer liquids. Both types offer high precision and reproducibility in liquid handling tasks, but differ in the mechanism of liquid transfer.</p></p>
