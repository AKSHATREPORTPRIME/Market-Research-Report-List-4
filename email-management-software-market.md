<p><p>Email management software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based email management software operates on a web-based platform and is hosted on the software provider's servers, allowing users to access it through an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises email management software is installed and managed on the user's local servers, giving them more control over data security and customization. Both types offer different benefits and considerations based on the organization's needs and preferences.</p></p>
