<p><p>X-ray Inspection Systems market can be categorized into two main types: Digital Radiography (DR) Type and Computed Tomography (CT) Type. </p><p>Digital Radiography (DR) Type systems use digital detectors to capture X-ray images, offering higher image quality and faster image processing compared to traditional film-based systems.</p><p>Computed Tomography (CT) Type systems provide 3D imaging by taking multiple X-ray images from different angles and reconstructing them into detailed cross-sectional images, allowing for better detection of internal defects and irregularities in the inspected objects.</p></p>
