<p><p>The KVM Extender market is divided into two main types: extender pairs and receiver markets. Extender pairs consist of transmitter and receiver units that work together to extend the distance between a computer and a keyboard, video monitor, and mouse. Receiver markets, on the other hand, involve the sale of standalone receiver units that can be connected to existing KVM extension setups to expand their capabilities. Both types cater to the increasing demand for efficient and flexible remote access solutions in various industries.</p></p>
