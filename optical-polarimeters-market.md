<p><p>Optical polarimeters are devices used to measure the polarization of light. There are two main types of optical polarimeters: single wavelength and double wavelength. Single wavelength polarimeters measure the degree of polarization of light at a single specific wavelength, while double wavelength polarimeters measure the polarization difference between two different wavelengths. Single wavelength polarimeters are simpler and more cost-effective, while double wavelength polarimeters provide more accurate and reliable measurements by taking into account wavelength-dependent effects on polarization.</p></p>
