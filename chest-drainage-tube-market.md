<p><strong>The Chest Drainage Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Tube</li><li>Multiple Chamber Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chest drainage tubes are classified into two main types - single chamber tubes and multiple chamber tubes. Single chamber tubes are simple in design and consist of a single collection chamber for draining fluid from the chest cavity. On the other hand, multiple chamber tubes have two or more compartments which allow for separate collection and monitoring of fluid and air. Both types of tubes are widely used in medical settings for managing conditions such as pneumothorax or pleural effusion.</p></p>
