<p><p>Fixed Pressure CPAP Devices deliver a constant level of air pressure throughout the night to treat sleep apnea. This type of CPAP machine is ideal for individuals who require a specific pressure setting. Auto Adjusting CPAP Devices, on the other hand, automatically adjust the air pressure based on the patient's breathing patterns. This type of CPAP machine is often preferred for individuals whose air pressure needs may vary throughout the night. Both market types offer effective treatment for sleep apnea.</p></p>
