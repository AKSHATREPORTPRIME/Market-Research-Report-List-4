<p><p>Non-Invasive Prenatal Testing (NIPT) is a type of prenatal testing that screens for common chromosomal conditions in a fetus. The market types for NIPT include ultrasound, Next Generation Sequencing (NGS), Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), and Microarray. Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of the fetus, while NGS analyzes DNA sequences to detect abnormalities. PCR amplifies and analyzes DNA samples, and Microarray detects genetic variations. Each of these market types plays a crucial role in providing accurate and reliable prenatal testing for expecting parents.</p></p>
