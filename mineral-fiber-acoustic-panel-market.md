<p><p>Mineral fiber acoustic panels come in various types, including glasswool, stonewool, rockwool, slagwool, glass filaments, ceramic fibers, and other variations. Glasswool panels are made from fine fibers of glass, while stonewool and rockwool panels are derived from natural rock materials. Slagwool is manufactured from blast furnace slag. Glass filament panels use glass fibers for insulation. Ceramic fiber panels are composed of ceramic materials. Other variations may include unique combinations or different manufacturing processes to create effective acoustic panels for various applications.</p></p>
