<p><p>Horizontal carousels in the market can be classified into two main types: hydraulic and mechanical. Hydraulic carousels operate using fluid power to control movement, providing smooth and precise positioning of items. On the other hand, mechanical carousels utilize gears, pulleys, and chains to rotate shelves and transport goods. Both types offer efficient storage and retrieval solutions, but the choice between hydraulic and mechanical carousels depends on factors such as load capacity, speed, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
