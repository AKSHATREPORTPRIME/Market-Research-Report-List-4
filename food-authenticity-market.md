LC-MS/MS stands for liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry, which is a technique used to analyze complex food samples for authenticity. Isotope market involves measuring the isotopic composition of food samples to assess their origin and authenticity. These methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and authenticity of food products.</p></p>
