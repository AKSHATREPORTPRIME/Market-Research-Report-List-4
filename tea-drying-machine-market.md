<p><p>Tea drying machines are typically categorized into two main types: tea frying dryers and tea baking dryers. Tea frying dryers use high temperatures to quickly remove moisture from tea leaves, while tea baking dryers use a slower baking process to gently dry the leaves. Both types of machines have their own unique advantages and are preferred by different tea manufacturers depending on the specific requirements of their products. The choice between these two types of machines depends on factors such as the desired flavor profile and level of tea leaf oxidation.</p></p>
